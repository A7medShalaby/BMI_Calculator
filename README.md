<h1 align="center">BMI_Calculator_App</h3>

## 🔍 Overview:

- 🐥 Used Dart & Flutter Framework.

- ☺ Creating Simple Ui With Satisfied Colors.

- 🌝 Easy To Use it.

- ✔️ Gives out The BMI Value & Categorizes BMI Based on Provided Information From WHO & CDC For Both Adults & Children.

- 🎤 Giving Healthy Advice After Calculating.



## 🎯 Features:

- 🚻 Switch Choosing Between Genders (Male & Female).

- ⬆️ Retractable Hight Slider Based on Your Hight.

- 🕦 Selecting Weight By Buttons.

- ⌛ Selecting Age By Buttons.

- 📲 Calculating All Input Data.

- 📶 Showing Your Data With 🎭 Expressive Image & 💡 Simple Advice.
